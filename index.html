Magic of Symmetry

"Magic of Symmetry" is my third assignment for the Introduction to Computational Arts MOOC offered by Coursera on fall 2014. It contains a blank canvas which displays instructions when first activated by the user. This interactive software allows the user to create a piece of artwork in collaboration with a computer. For each input, the computer will choose the colors and add the symmetry chosen by the user.
Press S to save, E to erase, + or - to change symmetry.

Source code: Symmetry
